Here you can read some useful things you can do if you want to save water

  • Turn off faucets.
  • Double-dip dishes.
  • Consider a smaller dishwasher.
  • Buy a high-efficiency washer.
  • Shorten your showers.
  • Use every drop.
  • Water by hand.
  • Capture rainwater.
  • Fill a jug with tap water and place this in your fridge.
  • Get a low-flush toilet.
  • Check your home from top to bottom for leaks.
  • Soak pots and pans before washing.
  • Defrost meat in the refridgerator overnight.
  • Reuse towels multiple times.
  • Wash your car the old fashion way.
