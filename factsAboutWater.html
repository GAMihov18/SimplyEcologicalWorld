Interesting facts about Water

  1. 97% of the water is salt water, only 3% is fresh.
  2. More water is found in the atmosphere than all of the planet combined.
  3. Hot water freezes faster than cold.
  4. Cold water is heavier than hot water.
  5. Water is the only substance on the earth which is found naturally in 3 forms: liquid, solid and gas.
